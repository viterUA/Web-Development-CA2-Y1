The way to it is very short if you come by train or bus, as it is located on the right side of the station. Nearby there are a number of stores such as Dunnes Store, Lild etc., also 5 metres from the guesthouse is a bike rental and if you follow the path behind the guesthouse you can take the GreenWay to Fenit to the beach, also a 10-minute walk to the theatre in the city centre, also a 5-minute walk to shopping centres, cafes, restaurants and pubs.

To book a room, we need to know your Name and Surname, Email address, the room you need, how many people will be staying, Arrival Date & Time, Departure Date, whether you need to be picked up from the airport, train or bus stop. There are Single room, Double room, Disability-friendly room, a restaurant, a playground, a conference room, a swimming pool, a gym. There is an immediate room check in for a people who booked online in advance, because the room was cleaned before your arrival, and for those who arrive and book a room on the spot, the check in takes place in 25 minutes, that is during this time room is cleaned.

This guest house have the Standart requests of the Tourist Traffic Acts 1939-2003 of Ireland, and also you can check our Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions as well.

If you need the Guide Dog, we have one and people with pets are allowed to stay.

About payment system Cash, Visa, MasterCard and American Express are allowed.

Smoking is restricted in any form, there is a separate area for smoking.

The weather here is mostly humid, with a lot of rain, windy, temperatures averaging from 0 to 16 degrees celsus, but it depends on the season.

Ireland has the Euro currency.
